Writing

Danielle has multiple books and manuscripts out to publishers. She is a regular contributor to blogs and memes. She is also a ghost writer.

One of her biggest contributions to writing regularly is NaNoWriMo, an organization dedicated to writing one novel per year in the month of November. Although Danielle doesn't often finish her novel by the deadline, the processes and workshops help her to flesh out a new novel idea for every year.
